Team Shatterdome Screen Play

Before the regionals, pre-matches between teams help both teams by sharing the feedback and tips, eventually finding the solutions for the problems altogether. Team Shatterdome highly values Gracious Professionalism, which is sharing the outcome of the team's efforts and passion of the season. For the teams that have difficulties to physically meet for the practice matches, video call-based matches will be the perfect rehearsal. Moreover, Korea only has the final match without regionals, so we deeply need keen contacts with other teams and pre-matches. Now, Team Shatterdome #8338 initiates this chance by inviting you to the SSG: Shatterdome Screen Game!
